Important information

These policies explain how PayPoint accounts and digital-service transactions are handled. They require legal approval before public launch.

Privacy notice

PayPoint processes account details, authentication records, phone numbers, payments, wallet entries, and service orders to operate the platform, prevent fraud, provide support, and meet legal obligations.

Your information

Information may be shared with payment, airtime, data, hosting, security, and compliance providers only as needed to deliver the service. Production retention periods and data-subject request procedures must be approved before launch.

Terms of use

Customers are responsible for checking phone numbers, networks, plans, and amounts before confirming a transaction. Completed digital delivery may not be reversible. Failed delivery after a confirmed wallet debit is investigated and refunded according to provider status.

Service availability

Provider maintenance and network conditions can delay delivery. Pending orders remain subject to reconciliation. Final commercial terms, fees, complaint timelines, and refund rules must be approved by qualified counsel.
